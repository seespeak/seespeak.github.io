How long will SeeSpeak take to arrive?

We aim to ship orders within 1 working day. Estimated delivery times are:

UK
1 - 2 working days
Europe
4 - 5 working days
Worldwide
from 5 - 7 working days

We send SeeSpeak out using the UK Royal Mail 'Signed For' service. Someone will need to sign for the parcel on delivery. If no one is in when a delivery is attempted, a 'Something For You' card will be put through your door. This will tell you how to arrange a redelivery or pick up the parcel from your local Royal Mail Delivery Office.

Will SeeSpeak work abroad / Is it available abroad?

SeeSpeak will work anywhere in the world you have a WiFi connection to the internet. The power adapter supplied as standard in the box is for UK and Europe plug sockets. We are happy to supply an extra power adapter for customers from outside Europe at no extra charge. Please let us know before you place your order and we can arrange for the correct power adapter to be added to your parcel.

We will ship SeeSpeak anywhere in the world. Please see the 'Postage' option next to the 'Buy Now' button for postage rates.

Can I download SeeSpeak as an app and install it on my own tablet? If not, could you install it on my tablet for me?

Unfortunately not. When our engineers install SeeSpeak on the tablet they also make changes to the tablet itself. These changes require specialist knowledge and tools that our customers are unlikely to have access to.

Additionally the changes we make to the tablet are specific to the hardware we supply and we could not guarantee they would on other devices.
