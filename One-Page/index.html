What is it?

SeeSpeak is a tablet-based device for making Skype video calls simply and intuitively, allowing you to easily see the person with whom you are talking.

How?

SeeSpeak displays large photographs of friends and family. With just one tap on a photograph, the user launches a Skype video call to that person.

Why is it needed?

SeeSpeak has been developed to connect generations living apart, especially those who find technology challenging.

Communication - Connection - Conversation

With a more mobile younger generation, many of us find ourselves living far from our parents and extended family. Video phones could bridge the gap, but are often too complicated for those whom technology has left behind. SeeSpeak has been designed to be easy to set up and easy to use, removing any worry for the technology wary user.

  • Make a video call with one tap
  • Uses Skype so you can easily connect to an existing large network of friends and family
  • Contacts are displayed as easy to see large photos and names
  • Photos and names are automatically downloaded from Skype or you can set your own
  • Comes with table top stand an long power lead so it can live in a prominent place in the home and always be charged
  • Family member can quickly set up and instruct the end user
  • Designed to be always on and ready to make and receive calls
  • Screen dims to low level when not being used
  • Single purpose; no opportunity to get lost in other app
  • No confusing pop-up messages or notifications
  • Silent software updates

How does it work?

Before Starting

A friend or family member of the user sets up a Skype account, adds in contacts and arranges access to Wi-Fi

Turning on

On turning on SeeSpeak for the first time, the helper is guided through connecting to the Wi-Fi and signing in to Skype

Ready to use

With simple guidance, the end user can now make video calls with maximum ease; stress free technology!

Accessible

Living in a prominent place, always charged and always on, calls can be made and received any time

What's in the box?

  • Acer 7" Tablet pre-installed with the SeeSpeak software
    The actual model may vary, but the minimum specifications will be a 600 x 1024 pixel screen, Dual Core 1.2 GHz, 1 GB Ram, 0.3M pixel front facing camera
  • Table-top stand
  • 1.8m USB cable and charger
  • Full paper instruction manual
  • WiFi is needed, but is NOT included
